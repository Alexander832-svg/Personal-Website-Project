Santa Ana


This is the place where i am from, Santa Ana, Sonora, is a Mexican municipality and the town that serves as its seat, located in the northwestern state of Sonora. It is known as the "key to the Great Altar Desert" ("la llave del gran desierto de Altar") and was founded in 1883 when the Sonoran Railroad was built, with roots tracing back to indigenous Pima settlements.
